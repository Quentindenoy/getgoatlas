Still curious?

Do I need design or video skills?

If you've used any modern video editor — timeline, keyframes, export — you already know enough. Go Atlas© uses the same model, applied to maps.

Can I use the exported videos commercially?

Yes. You own your videos. The map background is rendered with Mapbox, so the small Mapbox attribution stays visible in the corner of every export — that's required by their license. Same rule as Google Maps. Everything else is yours.

Do you collect my project data?

No. Your projects stay on your machine. We don't upload, store, or analyse the content of your scenes. Go Atlas© is GDPR-compliant; the only data we process is licensing (your key status) and support emails when you reach out. Full details in our Privacy Policy.

What machine do I need?

Go Atlas© is a real-time GPU-accelerated editor. It runs Mapbox, Three.js, and a 4K export pipeline in parallel — so it needs a creator-grade machine, not a budget office laptop.
